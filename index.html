Projects
The Park Planners

Worried if you can handle the newest rollercoaster? Curious what others think of your favorite ride? The Park Planners was a mix of my love of theme parks and planning, but my fear not knowing what to expect on a ride I haven't done before.

The idea behind the site is simple: aggregate what people think about how intense rides are. Users can make an account, then leave ratings and comments on rides in both the Disney World and Universal Orlando theme parks. This project is still early in the works, and I'm using it to teach myself Node.js and MongoDB.

Check it out!
